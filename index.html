Most Frequent Riding Month For Zagster Bike Rentles.

Data Visualization Project

Bar Chart


About Section:

This data was taken from https://zagster-service.herokuapp.com/rides/count/per_month, where it display's the total amount of rides per month, according to the zagster databse. After retreiving the data, it was put into a bar graph to create a visual persective for the data set. For this study, only the year 2017 will be used. The bar graph shows that the month of July had the most rides during the year, accumulating a total of 405 rides. While September was a close runner up, with 401 rides throughout that month. The data shows that during the 2017 year, people were more likley to use the Zagster bikes during the warmer months of the year.
